SYNOPSIS

Protagonist: Nick Telso Antagonist: DARPA/Government Co-Antagonist: Ming Supporting characters: Hallie, robots and the scientists, DARPA staff. Goal: to explore the unknown depths of the ocean and solve ancient and current mysteries of the deep. To utilize their water displacement machine “Archimedes.” Secondary goal: honor the agreement with Ming so the relatives of the scientists will not be killed by Ming Third goal: evade capture by DARPA who is intent on capturing or killing the scientists and Ming. Key features of the story is diversity of the cast from the ethnicity of its characters. Cool high tech equipment and cutting edge AI and Robotics.
